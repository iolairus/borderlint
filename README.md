# borderlint

**Map and govern where your AI data and traffic flow — with an east‑west / APAC lens.**

Everyone is wiring LLMs and agents into their applications. Far fewer can answer a question
their regulators are starting to ask: *where, and to whom, is our AI data flowing?*
`borderlint` aims to answer it from your codebase — a static, developer‑side check that maps
every AI provider you talk to, classifies each by jurisdiction, and governs those flows
against a residency policy you declare, in CI.

> **Status:** scoping. The project currently contains its **[capability set](CAPABILITIES.md)** —
> what borderlint should do — and no code yet. Feedback on scope welcome before implementation.
